The difference between web design and web development is often misunderstood. Web design focuses on the visual and aesthetic aspects of a website, creating a user-friendly interface and engaging layouts. On the other hand, web development involves the coding and programming required to bring the design to life, making the website functional and interactive. Both disciplines are essential for a successful website, and understanding their distinctions is crucial for effective collaboration between designers and developers. Keep up the good work!
