Can I update or change my beauty business's website after it is launched?

 

Yes, a good agency will give you login details to change or update content whenever you need to. This way, visitors always see the most up-to-date information and what's currently available.

Updating your website frequently is beneficial in two ways.

Firstly, it helps to keep your visitors engaged with fresh content.

Secondly, it improves visibility on search engines like Google as they prefer newer content.
