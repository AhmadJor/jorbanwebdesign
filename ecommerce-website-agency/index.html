What Is Shopify?

 

Shopify is a cloud-based ecommerce platform that lets businesses set up their own online stores quickly and easily. This gives them the freedom to reach out to customers in a variety of ways, such as through paid advertising, organic traffic, physical stores, and social media channels.

Everyone from retailers to independent entrepreneurs and start-ups can benefit from Shopify's powerful tools that allow for effortless integration with any other business systems or data sources.

What are the reasons to use Shopify?

 

Shopify is a powerful sales channel for ecommerce brands. It gives you powerful tools to automate and scale operations, making it easier to manage multiple sales channels and reach more customers.

Shopify also allows professional Shopify app development as well as custom app development for applications that are made to fit the needs of a specific business or industry niche. Through these custom solutions, businesses can not only increase their business growth, but also build their own custom digital experiences tailored specifically for user needs.

The advantages of using Shopify are numerous and include the following:

  • Quick and easy setup. Setting up and customizing an ecommerce store on Shopify requires no technical expertise or coding knowledge. With an intuitive user interface, you can get your store up and running quickly so you can start selling right away.
  • Secure payment processing. With built-in fraud protection and secure payment gateways, customers can be sure that their data is safe when making purchases from your store. Plus, no sensitive customer information is ever stored on Shopify’s servers, ensuring maximum security at all times.
  • 24/7 customer support. The dedicated team of experts at Shopify are available around the clock to help with any questions or issues you may have related to your shop. Whether it’s troubleshooting technical issues or offering advice on marketing strategies, they are always ready to assist.
  • Mobile-friendly design. Your shop will look great no matter what device a customer is viewing it on as each page is optimized for mobile devices. Customers can easily find what they’re looking for while shopping from their smartphones or tablets. This convenience will lead to increase in conversions and profitability.
  • Easy customization: With customizable themes and settings, you can easily tweak the look and feel of your shop without having to write any code yourself. This makes it easier than ever to alter the design and incorporate custom development whenever you want in order to keep up with trends or changes in product offerings.
  • Powerful Shopify marketing tools. To help improve your marketing campaigns, Shopify includes powerful marketing tools like email campaigns, discount codes, integration with social media platforms, analytics tracking and more—all accessible within one unified dashboard. With powerful analytics tracking capabilities in place, ecommerce growth has never been easier!
  • Search engine optimization (SEO). Shopify offers powerful SEO capabilities to support your digital presence. Whether you’re conducting national or international ecommerce, optimizing your Shopify store for search will open new doors and opportunities to grow your Shopify operations and increase sales.
  • Abandoned cart recovery service. If customers abandon items in their shopping carts without completing checkout, Shopify will send them emails reminding them about those items–helping increase conversion rates by reducing lost sales opportunities due to forgotten items left behind in carts!
  • Performance branding. Shopify gives you endless ways to brand your ecommerce store to your target demographic so that visitors will become customers.
  • Payment gateway. With its own proprietary payment gateway called “Shop Pay” built into every store, merchants have access to a secure solution that enables shoppers to quickly check out without leaving their shop!

What does a Shopify project plan does your agency provide and what does the store look like?

 

We take a customer-centric approach to ensure success when formulating our Shopify project plans. This lets us determine what drives customers and use that information to build a strategy.

Here are some of the issues that our Shopify development team focuses on before launching a Shopify project:

  1. Website performance. What is the loading time of the existing website across its different pages? Can it be improved upon?
  2. Conversions. What is the conversion rate? Is there a clear plan for how the redesign will increase conversions?
  3. Site structure. Did the old website design have a clear site architecture with intuitive navigation?
  4. Colour palette. Does the existing colour palette serve the brand, or must it be updated?
  5. Value proposition. Does the existing layout and website copy communicate the brand’s unique selling proposition, or must it be refreshed?
  6. Cart abandonment. What is the cart abandonment rate, and can it be improved upon? You want more people completing checkouts on your online store.
  7. Success stories. Does the website feature case studies prominently throughout?
  8. Bounce rates. Study user behaviour in Google Analytics. If visitors bounce off your site after a few seconds, you’re probably not articulating your value proposition enough. Or, you’re attracting the wrong visitors to your site.
  9. Authority and credibility. Does your existing site exude authoritativeness? Would you trust it if you were a stranger to it? You should have a plan for how the new version will improve upon marketing and sales aspects. From landing pages to product pages to your homepage, every part of your site should be aligned to strengthen your branding.
